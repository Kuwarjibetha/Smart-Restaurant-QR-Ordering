✨ Designed For Excellence

Everything for a modern dining experience

Powerful features built to delight customers and streamline kitchen operations — all in one system.

📱

Instant QR Scan & Order

Customers simply scan the table QR code with their smartphone camera to immediately view the menu and place orders. No app needed.

🤖

AI Meal & Budget Planner

Input budget limits or group sizes and let our intelligent AI suggest ideal dish combinations, dietary pairings, and allergen-safe meals.

🔥

Live Kitchen Progress

Customers see real-time updates as dishes move from Placed → Preparing → Ready. Kitchen staff control status via their Kanban board.
